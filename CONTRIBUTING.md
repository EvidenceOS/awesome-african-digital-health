# Contributing to Awesome African Digital Health

Thank you for your interest in contributing! This list is maintained by [PAATHI](https://paathi.org) and [EvidenceOS](https://evidenceos.com).

## Guidelines

- **One resource per pull request.** Include a short description and why it is relevant to African digital health.
- **For company additions:** Include AI type, clinical domain, evidence base, and regulatory status where known.
- **Maintain alphabetical order** within each section.
- **Verify all links** are working and point to canonical URLs.
- New categories are welcome if they contain 3+ entries.
- Flag outdated entries by opening an issue.

## Pull Request Format

    ### [Resource Name](https://example.com)

    Short description explaining what this resource is and its relevance to African digital health.

## For Company Landscape Entries

Please use this table row format:

    | [Company Name](URL) | Country | AI Type | Domain | Scale & Evidence | Regulatory Status | Interoperability |

## Quality Standards

- Resources must be actively maintained (updated within the last 2 years)
- Commercial products must have documented African deployment or evidence
- Research papers must be published in indexed journals or recognized preprint servers
- Tools and SDKs must be open source or have free-tier access for LMICs

## Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and focused on improving access to health technology across Africa.
